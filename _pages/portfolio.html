--- layout: archive title: "Portfolio" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} ### Project Overview The **COAST Guidewire** is the `world's smallest` tendon-driven robotic guidewire designed for high-precision endovascular interventions. This project integrates real-time shape sensing and force feedback with fiber bragg Grating sensors to improve localization and navigation in complex vascular networks. ### Robot Demonstration Below is the demonstration presented at the **Hamlyn Symposium on Medical Robotics**. ### Key Features * **Novel Design:** Novel steerable mechanism for reducing axial perforation. * **Integrated Sensing:** Real-time force and shape feedback for the surgeons and control systems. * **Clinical Impact:** Aimed at reducing radiation exposure, improving safety and reducing procedure time in neurosurgery
