--- layout: archive title: "Portfolio" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} ### Project Overview The **COAST Guidewire** is the `world's smallest` tendon-driven robotic guidewire designed for high-precision endovascular interventions. This project integrates real-time shape sensing and force feedback with **Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG)** sensors to improve localization and navigation in complex vascular networks. --- ### Robot Demonstration *Presented at the **Hamlyn Symposium on Medical Robotics***
--- ### Key Features * **Novel Design:** Small-scale steerable mechanism engineered specifically for **reducing axial perforation** during navigation. * **Integrated Sensing:** Real-time force and shape feedback loops designed for both **surgeon haptics** and **autonomous control systems**. * **Clinical Impact:** Target outcomes include reducing radiation exposure (fluoroscopy time), improving patient safety, and significantly lowering procedure duration in **neurosurgery**.
