Project Overview

The COAST Guidewire is the world's smallest tendon-driven robotic guidewire designed for high-precision endovascular interventions. This project integrates real-time shape sensing and force feedback with Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensors to improve localization and navigation in complex vascular networks.

Key Features

  • Novel Design: Novel steerable mechanism for reducing axial perforation.
  • Integrated Sensing: Real-time force and shape feedback for the surgeons and control systems.
  • Clinical Impact: Aimed at reducing radiation exposure, improving safety and reducing procedure time in neurosurgery.
